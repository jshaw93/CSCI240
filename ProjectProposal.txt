Project Overview

Web application will make use of a MySQL database consisting of 1,289 different Oldschool Runescape
item combinations that produce tradeable items.  12 categories for items, some items exist in 
multiple categories due to their uses.

DB Data

Types: 12 different categories of item combination data for use in web application
Entities: 1,289 separate item combinations denoted by the names of their produced item

DB use cases

Market monitoring for profitable methods to level skills, or just gain money.
How much profit might I make by creating a certain item?
How much would it cost to make n many items?
Certain combinations work with different mathematical equations to determine profit/loss, application
calculates with those different equations depending on category/item.
